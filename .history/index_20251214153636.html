Chrome Extension to Auto Track Packages from Gmail

If you're someone who orders online frequently and constantly loses track of when your packages will arrive, ShipMate is here to help. Our Chrome extension for Gmail package tracking automatically detects shipping notifications in your inbox and organizes all your delivery tracking information in one convenient dashboard. Unlike traditional package trackers that require you to manually enter tracking numbers, ShipMate works seamlessly in the background, scanning your emails and keeping you updated on every shipment from Amazon, USPS, FedEx, UPS, and dozens of other carriers. With smart ETA predictions, real-time status updates, and automatic delivery notifications, ShipMate takes the hassle out of package tracking so you can focus on what matters most. Frequently Asked Questions

How does ShipMate work?

ShipMate automatically scans your Gmail inbox for shipping notifications from major carriers and retailers. Once installed, it detects tracking numbers from emails and organizes them in a clean dashboard. You never need to manually enter tracking numbers - ShipMate does it all automatically in the background.

Is my Gmail data private and secure?

Yes, your privacy is our top priority. ShipMate is Google LOV (Letter of Validation) verified, meaning we've passed Google's strict security requirements. Your data never leaves your device except to fetch shipping updates, and we never sell or share your information with third parties. Learn more about Gmail API security and permissions.

Is ShipMate free to use?

Yes! ShipMate offers a free plan that tracks up to 2 active packages with basic features. For unlimited package tracking, detailed views, smart ETA predictions, and advanced filters, you can upgrade to ShipMate Pro for $4.99/month.

What carriers does ShipMate support?

ShipMate supports all major carriers including USPS, FedEx, UPS, DHL, Amazon Logistics, and many more. Our system automatically recognizes tracking numbers from hundreds of retailers and shipping providers worldwide.
